Generatative AI Book Cover

Tool(s): ChatGPT, Adobe Firefly, and Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: ChatGPT was used to come up with the title, subtitel, and author. The group I was in wanted to do a halloween or horror adjacent genre for the book cover. After generating the image, the book cover was imported to Photoshop, where I came up with the right placement for the text, and typed in the text for the book cover.
