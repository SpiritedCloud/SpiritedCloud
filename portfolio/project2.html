Movie Poster

Tool(s): Illustrator

Project Description: I used Ilustrator's vector tools and it's pattern tools for this project. I read through the short story of "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty," and interpreted a movie poster through the scenes and themes of the story. I used the color purple, which was a color that came to mind as I brainstormed, and yellow was used as the complementary color. I also looked at movie posters that already existed for an actual movie based on this short, which featured the sky and mountains, which symbolised the main character's head being up in the clouds.
