12 Months of Mirrors

Tool(s): Premiere Pro

Project Description: The video was edited using Premiere Pro, and the clips used in the video were all found online. The music that plays in the background is from the genre of Electro swing, and is created by the Caravan Palace. Each clip was zoomed in to fit the screen without strecthing it. I also used red text so it would easily contrast with the clips, and used this shade of red because it felt complimentary to the vintage feel of the video.
