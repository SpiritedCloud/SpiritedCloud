Geometric Glitch Collage

Tool(s): Hex Fiend, and Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: This is glitch art made using an image of the Moulin Rouge found on the internet, and glitched through messing with it's code using Hex. The colors on the image are mostly the exact same as the original, with the other colors coming after it has been glitched. I then put the image into photoshop, and copy and pasted the glitches on other parts of the photo.
