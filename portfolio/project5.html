Species Design

Tool(s): Adobe Photoshop

Project Description: The new species is a combination of a deer, a rabbit, a bird, and a tv. All images were found online. I combied these images using Photoshop, I used the selection tools to copy and paste different parts of the photos that I wanted to use, and composited them together. I then color corrected it to the colors that I think seemed best.
